About Us

Started in 2017 with the passion to serve in the area of water e-governance. The motto of the company is to regulate water management through the IoT platform. The Company named Bulfro is derived from the animal Bull Frog which is found in eastern North America and this amphibian rarely sleeps. Resembling this quality, the tagline was marked as Amphibian That Never Sleeps since all the products are connected to a cloud server that never sleeps. 

Since its inception Bulfro has developed Solar Pumps, Digital Water Level Recorders, Pump Health Monitoring and Control systems, Flow Monitech, and Automated Rain Gauge, Water Management Systems all of them are telemetry enabled.
Bulfro’s business activities can be characterized by quality, innovation, timeliness, competitive awareness, ethical business practices, and outstanding service to our customers.
We believe everything is achievable by having faith in our product, services and hard work.
